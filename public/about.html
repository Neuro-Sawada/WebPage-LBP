About Lilly Brothers Painting

Lilly Brothers Painting is a family-owned painting company proudly serving Cincinnati and surrounding areas. We focus on quality craftsmanship, clear communication, and professional service from the first quote to the final walkthrough.

Our team handles interior painting, exterior painting, drywall repair, prep work, and detailed finishes. Whether it is one room, a full home, or a commercial space, we treat every project with care and attention to detail.

We believe a great paint job is more than just color on a wall. It is about proper preparation, clean lines, reliable scheduling, and results that make your space feel refreshed and well finished.
